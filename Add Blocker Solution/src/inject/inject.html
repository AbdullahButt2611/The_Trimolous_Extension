Add Blocker Solution

Instructions

  1. First Click on the Ellipses (Vertical three dots) given at top right corner of chrome. A drop down menu will appear

  2. Hover over to the "More Tools" options and a sub-menu will again appear. Choose the "Extension" option.

  3. An extension manager must have opened. Now search for the extension named "Add Blocker".

  4. Then click on the "Remove" button right below the option

  5. Confirm the deletion process.
